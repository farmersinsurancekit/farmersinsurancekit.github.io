Common Challenges and How to Overcome Them

Delays in Processing

One of the most frequent complaints is delayed claim settlements. This often happens due to incomplete documentation or miscommunication. To avoid this:
- Double-check all submitted forms.
- Keep digital copies of every document.
- Maintain a record of all interactions with the insurer.

Claim Rejections

Claims can be denied for reasons like policy exclusions or non-disclosure of pre-existing conditions. To minimize rejection risks:
- Read your policy terms carefully before purchasing.
- Disclose all relevant medical history upfront.
- Seek clarification from Star Health’s customer service if unsure about coverage.
